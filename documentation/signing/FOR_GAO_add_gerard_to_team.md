# For Gao: Add Gerard to Apple Developer Team

This is the **recommended approach** - Gerard gets his own certificate and can manage builds independently.

---

## Step 1: Add Gerard as a Team Member

1. Go to [Apple Developer Portal](https://developer.apple.com/account)
2. Sign in with: `gao.kabubi@gmail.com`
3. Click **People** in the left sidebar (or go to Account → People)
4. Click the **+** button to invite a new member
5. Enter Gerard's details:
   - **Email**: *(Gerard's Apple ID email)*
   - **Role**: Select **Admin** (can create certificates and manage app IDs)
6. Click **Invite**

Gerard will receive an email invitation to join the team.

---

## Step 2: That's It!

Once Gerard accepts the invitation, he can:
- Create his own "Developer ID Application" certificate
- Sign and notarize builds from his Mac
- Manage the app independently

---

## Alternative: Export Your Certificate (Option B)

If you prefer not to add Gerard to the team, you can export your certificate instead. See the document `FOR_GAO_export_certificate.md`.
